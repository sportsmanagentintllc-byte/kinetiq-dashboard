The Problem

Africa's talent is the world's most valuable — and least financially protected — export.

Hundreds of millions in signing fees, training compensation, and remittances disappear to hidden FX margins, unaudited agents, and infrastructure that was never built for this market. KINETIQ closes that gap.

Hidden FX Margins
Banks claim transfers are free then apply rates 10–15% below market. KINETIQ shows every number — market rate, our rate, our exact fee — before you confirm.
Unverifiable Agent Commissions
FIFA FFAR capped agent commissions at 10% — but no system enforces it. KINETIQ does, automatically, on every transaction, with a permanent audit trail.
Grassroots Clubs Never Paid
The FIFA Clearing House owes African academies billions in solidarity payments — but clubs lack compliant banking rails to receive them. KINETIQ is that infrastructure.
No Financial Rails for Informal Talent
Before a formal contract, athletes have no compliant way to receive, hold, or move money internationally. KINETIQ creates that layer from day one of discovery.
